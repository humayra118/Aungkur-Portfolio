A little about me

I am enough of an artist to draw freely upon my imagination. The point is that when I see a sunset or a waterfall or something, for a split second it's so great, because for a little bit I'm out of my brain, and it's got nothing to do with me. I am currently studying at Rajshahi Medical College. In my free time I do it. I love photography and my hobby is reading book. Humayun ahmed and Sadat Hossain is my favorite writer.
